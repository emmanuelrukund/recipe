Stuffed Mushrooms

By Abdirizak Abdi

Mexican Stuffed Mushrooms are a delicious appetizer or snack that incorporates flavors inspired by Mexican cuisine. While there can be variations in the recipe, the dish typically involves stuffing mushroom caps with a flavorful mixture of ingredients.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 24 mushrooms, rinsed and hollowed
  • 24 mushrooms stems, chopped finely
  • 9-ounces pork chorizo
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1/3 cup Guacamole Salsa
  • 3 ounces cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 3/4 cup panko crumbs

PROCEDURE:

  1. In medium saucepan, cook chorizo, onion and garlic for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Stir in HERDEZ® Guacamole Salsa, cream cheese, parsley and cilantro. Cook 1-2 minutes, or until cheese is melted and well combined.
  3. Stuff each mushroom generously and place in greased cast iron skillet. Top with panko crumbs.
  4. Cook at 400° for 20 minutes.
