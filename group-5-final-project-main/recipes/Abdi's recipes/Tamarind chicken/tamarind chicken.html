Tamarind Chicken

By Abdirizak Abdi

Tamarind Chicken otherwise known as Chicken wings with Guajillo Chile Tamarind Sauce is a flavorful and spicy dish that features chicken wings coated in a sauce made with Guajillo chilies and tamarind.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2-pounds chicken wings, cooked
  • ½ cup Guajillo Pepper Paste
  • ¼ cup Chipotle Pepper Paste
  • ¾ cup ketchup
  • ½ cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup Tamarind concentrate

PROCEDURE:

  1. In a pot, over medium heat, cook all ingredients for 10 minutes, or until sugar is dissolved and flavors blend.
  2. Toss wings in Tamarind Chile Sauce.
  3. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds.
