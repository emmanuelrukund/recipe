Enchiladas

By AnnJoy Kendi

An enchilada is a mexican dish consisting of a corn tortilla rolled around a filling and covered with a savory sauce. Enchiladas can be filled with various ingredients includung;meats, cheese, beans, potatoes and vegetables.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Tortillas
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Cooked beef/chicken/beans
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Jalapenos
  • cilantro
  • Sour cream

PROCEDURE:

  1. Prepare the oven by heating it to about 175 degrees
  2. Prepare your desired filling whether its beef chicken or beans and any vegetables you would like to like and season them with salt,pepper or any spices of your liking
  3. Warm the tortillas in an oven to make them more pilable to prevent thm from tearing when you roll them
  4. Pour the enchilada sause on the assembeled enchiladas covering them evenly
  5. Sprinkle some cheese on top of the enchildas to enhance the flavor
  6. Place tehe baking dish into the pre heated oven for about 20-25 minutes until all the enchiladas are heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly
  7. Garnish and serve after removing the enchiladas from thr oven and let the cool slightly. Garnish withe the chopped cilantro and some sour cream
  8. If desired, serve hot and Enjoy!!
