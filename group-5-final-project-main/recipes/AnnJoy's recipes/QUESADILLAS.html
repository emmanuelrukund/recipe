Cheesy Quesadillla

By AnnJoy Kendi

A quesadilla is a mexican dish consisting of a tortilla that is filled primarily with cheese and sometimes meats, spices and other fillings and then cooked on a griddle or stove.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Cooked protein eg: beef/chicken/shrimp
  • Vegetables eg:onions,tomatoes
  • Salsa or diced tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Avocado/guacamole
  • Jalapenos
  • Refried beans
  • Cilantro

PROCEDURE:

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Pre heat the pan and place it on a stove on medium heat, let it warm up as you prepare the quesadillas
  3. Place a tortilla on a flat surface and add a layer of cheese evenly across the tortillas leaving some space around the edges for melting
  4. Add the fillings such as:cooked meat,beans,vegetables on top of the cheese
  5. Add another layer of cheese on top of the fillings, this helps in holding everything together when the quesadillas are cooked
  6. Top up with a second tortilla on top of the filling and press down gently to sandwich everything together
  7. Carefully transfer the assembled quesadillas to the pre heated pan.Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until the tortillas are golden brown
  8. Use a spatula to flip the quesadilla halfway through cooking to ensure that both sides are cooked evenly
  9. Once both sides are golden brown and cheese has melted, remove the quesadillas from the pan andd place on a cutting board,allow to cool and then slice
  10. Serve with your favorite toppings such as guacamole,salsa or chopped fresh cilantro
